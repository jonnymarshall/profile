RemoteKick

RemoteKick allows students, digital nomads and all types of remote workers to find the perfect cafe or coworking space. Users can search for spaces with a host of useful filters such as wifi speeds, comfort and plug socket availability. Venue data is continuously updated based on data provided by users.
