About Me

After studying Cinema and Photography at the University of Leeds, UK, I was eager to pack up my things, move to London, and kick off my career as a creative filmmker in London's thriving creative industy.
After a fantastic six years working on everything from TV commercials, online content, pitch films, social campaigns, and everything in between, I began to realise that there was an itch that I couldn't quite scratch - my deep love of technology.
I have always been obsessed with apps, startups, innovative technology, and I am a big proponent of cryptocurrency and the sharing economy.
After spending some time trying to work with others to build app and website ideas that were burning away in my imagination, I finally realised that if I wanted to shift into the tech world, it was time to learn to code. So, at the end of 2018, I packed up my things and jetted off to Lisbon to complete the LeWagon full-stack coding bootcamp.
Since then I have been traveling around Asia whilst working away on a few projects, spending countless hours buried in code and drowning in stack overflow tabs!
Thankfully this has given me the opportunity to build up my portfolio and given me great confidence that I am ready to join a talented team, contribute my skills and continue to learn and progress in this exciting industry.

Borrowbox

Borrowbox uses a unique system which rewards people with credits for lending, which can in turn be used to borrow from others. This system encourages a delicate balance between lending and borrowing. Don't want to lend out your stuff? No problem, you can buy credits quickly and easily in just one click.

WifiWarriors

WifiWarriors allows students, digital nomads and all types of remote workers to find the perfect cafe or coworking space. Users can search for spaces with a host of useful filters such as wifi speeds, comfort and plug socket availability. Venue data is continuously updated based on data provided by users.

Parkermate

Parkermate (concept only) is a tool for matching drivers with the perfect parking space, allowing drivers to easily see which roads they can park on, how much it costs, and estimated availability. It can even account for nearby local transport, so drivers can park and ride with ease and at minimal cost.
