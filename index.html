I'm Jonny Marshall.

I'm a junior fullstack Ruby on Rails developer fresh out of coding bootcamp and ready to start tackling the world of code. As a former filmmaker, motion graphics designer and editor who created films for some of the biggest national and international companies for six years working in the heart of London's creative industry, I am ready to apply my skills as a visual storyteller and creative to the tech world. Learn more about me.

About Me

After studying Cinema and Photography at the University of Leeds, UK, I was eager to pack up my things, move to London, and kick off my career as a creative filmmker in London's thriving creative industy.
After a fantastic six years working on everything from TV commercials, online content, pitch films, social campaigns, and everything in between, I began to realise that there was an itch that I couldn't quite scratch - my deep love of technology.
I have always been obsessed with apps, startups, innovative technology, and I am a big proponent of cryptocurrency and the sharing economy.
After spending some time trying to work with others to build app and website ideas that were burning away in the background, I finally realised that if I wanted to shift into the tech world, it was time to learn to code. So at the end of 2018 I packed up my things and jetted off to Lisbon, Portugal to complete the LeWagon Full Stack Coding bootcamp.
Since then I have been traveling around Asia around whilst working away on some personal projects, spending countless hours buried in code and stack overflow tabs!
Thankfully this has given me the opportunity to build up my portfolio and given me great confidence that I am ready to join a team, contribute my skills and continue to learn and progress in this exciting industry.
